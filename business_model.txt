Business Model 
Social Media Promotion Marketplace 
Users buy promo points (pp)
Use the (pp) as platform currency to promote their social medias or business 
Users click on the links and fulfill the tasks listed by other users, the (pp) credited to them
Any who have (pp) can use it to promote links if approved by the platform admin 
No (pp) no access to promote links
